Financing Policies

We offer flexible financing options to make solar energy more accessible

  • Cash Payment: Receive a 5% discount on your total bill when you pay upfront.
  • Loan Financing: Work with one of our trusted financing partners to pay in monthly installments.
  • Leasing: If you'd prefer not to own your solar panels, we offer a leasing option where you pay a low monthly fee.

Delivery Policies

  • Free Shipping:We provide free delivery for all solar panel purchases within 50 miles of your installation address.
  • Installation Timing:Most installations are completed within 7-14 business days after order confirmation.
  • Tracking:Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number for real-time updates.

Return Policies

  • 30-Day Return:If you're not satisfied with your solar panel purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund. (Conditions apply)
  • Warranty:All our solar panels come with a 25-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects and performance issues.
